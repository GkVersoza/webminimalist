What Should I Expect From My First Visit?

• Why are you visiting?
• Are you or have you been injured?
• How long have you been in pain?
• Have you seen any providers or therapists for this issue?
• Are you taking any medications to treat this? Any others?
• Do you take vitamins or herbal medications?
• Have you broken any bones or had any fractures?
• Have you missed work because of this problem? How much?
• On a scale of 1-10 (10 being the worst) what level is your pain?
• Is there anything else you would like to discuss?
